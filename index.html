Biography

My journey started as a child, learning from my father and brother, nine years ago I embraced tattooing, using it to express emotions. With styles like Negative Light, Paletting, Abstract, and Surrealism.

My art has been showcased globally, including at Carréousel du Louvre and World Art Dubai. Painting & art is depth to my vision, helping you to express yourself.

Through my unique art, I invite the observer on a journey of self-knowledge and personal expression, where each work represents a bridge between the artist and those who contemplate it.
